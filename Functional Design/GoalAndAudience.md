**Who is the user?**

This application is designed for individuals who are passionate about music and seek to learn, improve, or further develop their guitar-playing skills. The target users include both beginners and intermediate players who value structured learning, guided practice, and continuous skill development.

**why does the user want this product**

The user wants this application because they value progression and consistent feedback while learning to play the guitar or improving their existing skills. The app serves as a structured guideline, helping the user track their progress, stay motivated, and practice more effectively.


## Goal

The goal of this application is to guide individuals who enjoy playing the guitar, as well as those who aspire to learn how to play, in a structured and effective direction that supports skill improvement and the acquisition of new techniques. The application aims to stimulate enthusiasm for music-making while promoting continuous learning and the development of new skills.

**Technical goal**

The technical goal of this application is to establish a clean, robust, and scalable software architecture. This architecture should support future growth and flexibility, enabling potential expansion to include additional musical instruments or the integration of new concepts and functionalities.
